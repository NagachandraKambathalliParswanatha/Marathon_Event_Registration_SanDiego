Marathon, Half, 10K and Relay

An annual running tradition for the past decade here in one of Southern California’s most naturally beautiful and picturesque cities, San Diego’s storied SDSU Marathon added the 10-km marathon race distance on 3rd December 2017 starts at 8:30am.Rock out at the SDSU Marathon! This 10 kms marathon and 5 kms half-marathon features live music lining the streets, cheer squads, and a huge post-marathon concert to end the race with a bang. With more than 2,000 runners and 10,000 enthusiastic spectators, this popular event is not to be missed!

Finish Line amenities

All participants will receive a race shirt, finishers medal, post-race refreshments,Mylar blankets, chocolate milk, Nom Nom food bag and more!Finish Line Festival Beer Garden!Finishers can enjoy a complimentary Ballast Point beer! and a truly unforgettable race experience.
